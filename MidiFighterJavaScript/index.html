The Story

This project was inspired by the amazing Shawn Wasabi and his song "Marble Soda" that he performs with a custom Midi Fighter 64 built by Michael Mitchell and multiple samples from 153 different sources. In 2017, they found a manufacturer to produce a limited number of Midi Fighter 64s to sell, but sadly I couldn't afford one. So I did the next best thing... I made one... with JavaScript. Enjoy!
